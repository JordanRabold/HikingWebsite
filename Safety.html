Safety

After you've gathered your gear and you're ready to head out on your first trail, don't forget the "10 Essentials". What are the 10 essentials you ask? The 10 essentials are a list of important gear you should carry to keep you safe on every hike no matter how long the length. Down below I have a quick video going more in depth on the 10 essentials and a little extra to bring along your journey.
